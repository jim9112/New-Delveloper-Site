Documentation of the Development Process

This page was created for my fictional uncle Kyle. The goal was to create a simple web page that that promoted Kyles business and reflects my fictional uncles love of the state of New Hampshire. The site needs to explain what uncle Kyle’s business does, why the customer should do business with him, and a way to contact him if interested in learning more. This site was an attempt to encapsulate all of those needs.

Defense of the Final Product

The main home page has basic information about the kind of business that Kyle runs, along with an embeded video that highlights why small businesses can benefit from the kind of service that Kyle provides. The website includes a contact page which allows the user to fill out a form requesting to be contacted by Kyle for further information. The next page that is included gives testimonials from other customers to help persuade potential customers to utilize Kyles services. There is also a page that provides links to resources for business owners on other sites. The design of the site is cohesive, the background, colors and fonts are the same across each page. Each page also has the menus, main header, and main content located in the same spots. This allows the user to easily transition from page to page and quickly find what they are looking for.

Opportunities for Improvement and Growth

In the future incorporating social media into the site may help with growth. Also, because each page does not contain a lot of content switching to a single page design may be more effective and more in keeping with current design treneds.
